About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by the visionary partnership of Chef Antonio Rossi and restaurateur Maria Lopez, Café Fausse was born from a passion for blending traditional Italian flavors with modern culinary innovation. Our mission has always been to provide more than just a meal; we strive to create an unforgettable dining experience that reflects both quality and creativity in every dish.

The Founders

Chef Antonio Rossi

Co-Founder & Head Chef

A master of his craft, Chef Antonio Rossi spent years honing his skills in the kitchens of Milan and Rome. His philosophy is simple: honor the classic techniques while pushing the boundaries of flavor. At Café Fausse, he transforms locally sourced ingredients into culinary masterpieces that delight and surprise.

Maria Lopez

Co-Founder & Restaurateur

With an exceptional eye for design and a passion for hospitality, Maria Lopez creates the ambiance that makes every visit to Café Fausse so special. She believes that the atmosphere is just as important as the food, ensuring that from the moment you step inside, you feel welcomed and cared for.

Our Commitment

Unforgettable Dining

Every visit is a complete sensory experience, from the ambiance to the final bite.

Excellent Food

Our chefs craft each dish with precision, passion, and a dedication to exquisite flavor.

Locally Sourced

We partner with local farmers and suppliers to ensure the freshest, highest-quality ingredients.
